About Us

Our Vision: Transforming communities and youth lives by connecting young people to economic opportunities and dignity, together building a future that works.

Our Mission: To create work opportunities for South African youth at scale by connecting them with companies. We aim to enhance youth employability through the provision of CVs, reference letters, and transitioning them into earning future incomes, leveraging the B-BBEE policy for a meaningful impact.

Governance

We prioritize strong governance and processes, achieving four years of clean audits.

  • Annual internal audit conducted by BDO
  • Clean audited financial statements:
    • FYE 2021
    • FYE 2022
    • FYE 2023
  • Rigorous policy and governance structures
  • Audit and Risk Committee
  • Annual external audit conducted by Ernst and Young

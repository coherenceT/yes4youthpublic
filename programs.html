Our Programs

Explore our various programs designed to uplift and empower young people.

YES Digitate Lab

A digital training lab offering a range of specialized digital learning programs. Training partners include Vodacom, Google, Microsoft, and IT Varsity, equipping youth with essential skills for the digital age.

Green Engine Aquaponics and Farming

Engages youth in hydroponics programs and commercial farms where they learn about urban agriculture and sustainability while selling their produce in local markets.

YES Drone Academy

Provides candidates with training to repair, operate, and fly drones, equipping them to offer services in key sectors of the economy such as agriculture, logistics, and surveillance.

YES Culinary Academy

Offers chef training and entrepreneurial incubation through live restaurants where youth learn culinary skills, food safety, and restaurant management.

YES Textile Manufacturing Centre

Focuses on training and upskilling seamstresses and manufacturers, connecting them to networks to develop and find retail markets for their products.

YES Small Business Support Centre

The YES Small Business Support Centre is dedicated to empowering aspiring entrepreneurs by providing comprehensive business training programs. Participants receive mentorship from experienced business leaders and access to a wide range of resources. This initiative helps young entrepreneurs develop essential skills in business planning, marketing, financial management, and operations, ensuring they are equipped to launch and sustain successful businesses.

YES Automotive Academy

The YES Automotive Academy offers comprehensive hands-on training in automotive repair and maintenance, specifically designed to equip youth with the practical skills needed for successful careers in the automotive industry. Our program covers a wide range of topics, including vehicle diagnostics, engine repair, electrical systems, and brake systems. Participants learn in a state-of-the-art workshop, gaining real-world experience working on various vehicle models.

In addition to technical skills, the academy emphasizes the importance of safety standards and best practices in automotive service. Trainees also receive career development support, including resume workshops, interview preparation, and job placement assistance, helping them transition smoothly into the workforce. With strong partnerships in the automotive industry, graduates have the opportunity to connect with potential employers and secure internships or full-time positions in reputable automotive companies.

By completing the YES Automotive Academy program, youth not only gain valuable skills but also increase their employability, paving the way for successful careers in a growing sector that offers diverse opportunities for advancement.

YES Ceramics and Pottery Lab

The YES Ceramics and Pottery Lab provides comprehensive training in ceramics and pottery, enabling youth to explore their creativity and craftsmanship while creating unique, handcrafted products. Participants learn various techniques, including wheel throwing, hand-building, glazing, and firing, under the guidance of skilled artisans. The program fosters an appreciation for art and design while equipping youth with practical skills that can be applied in both artistic and commercial contexts.
